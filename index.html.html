About EMATOHO

Born from experience.
Built for Eswatini.

EMATOHO — meaning work in siSwati — was founded by a Built Environment professional with 15 years of infrastructure management experience at an international airport. Having navigated the challenges of professional growth firsthand, EMATOHO was built to be the bridge that candidates and employers deserve.

"We combine human expertise with AI precision to deliver shortlists HR teams can trust."

Operating under a registered investment company and grounded in Eswatini, EMATOHO serves local companies, government bodies, and international employers seeking African talent — with full-service recruitment from listing through placement.

Our Mission

Connect the right talent to the right opportunity with speed, accuracy and integrity.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted recruitment partner in Eswatini and the region.
